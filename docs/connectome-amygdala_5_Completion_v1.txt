PART ONE - DELIVERED

The valence and salience region is delivered and green. Seven packs (two
reused-verbatim substrate packs and five stratum packs) implement appraise,
condition, and modulate as internal sub-modules of the region stratum, on a
supporting cortisol receptor cascade (macromolecular), a pair of projection neurons
(cellular), an associative plasticity write (synaptic), and the community stratum as
the social source of cortisol. The region APPRAISES a stimulus - it assigns a
valence (an affective tag) and a salience (how much it matters), it learns threat by
association (fear conditioning), and it modulates the appraisal by cortisol. This is
a genuinely new job: the earlier regions selected (arbiter), stored (hippocampus),
and corrected (cerebellum); none had to appraise.

The committed appraisal is guarded by PrologAI's membership contract, declared in
its accessor form (a membership-test goal, so the legal set is never materialised)
and in once mode (one appraisal per stimulus, checked deterministically). The legal
valence set is {threat, safe, appetitive, neutral} plus the explicit abstention
no_appraisal, and it is CONTEXT-DEPENDENT: under stress the appetitive valence is
suppressed and is not legal, while the salience is a continuous real in the unit
interval - so the legal set is neither a fixed list nor even countable, which is
exactly the case the accessor form and the once mode were built for. The safety
property, no undefined valence, is therefore a one-line declaration, not a
hand-rolled guard.

The runner narrates, glass-box, all four valences and the abstention, the cortisol
modulation (food under stress reads neutral rather than appetitive; a
part-conditioned tone under stress reads threat), and three conditioning episodes: a
paired tone acquires threat, converging at five trials (the association climbs from
0.0 to 0.969 and the cue flips neutral to threat); a conditioned tone extinguishes,
converging at five trials (0.9 down to 0.028); and an alternating partial-
reinforcement schedule bounded-stops at eight trials (the association resting near
0.33, a legal "not fully conditioned"). The adversarial attempts to commit an
invented valence and an appetitive appraisal under stress are both refused.

PART THREE - A BUG FOUND AND FIXED

The build surfaced one defect and fixed it in place. The associative write
synaptic_stratum_potentiate/3 left a spurious choicepoint - first-argument indexing
keys on the association strength, not on the true/false pairing, so the clause head
did not commit - which raised a Prolog Unit (PLUnit) "succeeded with choicepoint"
warning. It was fixed with a green cut (number(Assoc0), ! at the head of each
clause), making the write deterministic. No behaviour changed: the association's
values are identical before and after, and every suite stays green.

PART FIVE - HONEST LIMITS

The region's honest limits are its two open Ledger findings. They are the product of
the program, not defects to hide.

  AMYGDALA-1 (severity high, the headline, a new gap with no parent): PrologAI has no
  first-class construct for a persisted, modulatory affective state. Two concrete
  walls follow. First, the membership contract - even its accessor form - guards the
  OUTPUT value alone: its test goal is called once(call(TestGoal, Out)) and cannot
  receive context, so to make the legal valence set depend on the cortisol regime the
  region must SMUGGLE the regime into the committed value, carrying the triple
  appraisal(Valence, Salience, Regime) rather than a pair. Second, the persisted
  affect - the learned association and the cortisol tone - is carried as ordinary
  Lattice and module data (plain fields on the conditioning-loop state), not as a
  first-class held-affect construct. The honest workaround, recorded as a workaround,
  is to fold the regime into the term and carry affect as data. The minimum remedy is
  a first-class affective-state or modulatory-context construct plus a context-aware
  accessor of the shape call(TestGoal, Out, Context). This is the finding the program
  predicted and chose an appraising region to surface; selection, storage, and
  correction never had to hold an affective state.

  AMYGDALA-2 (severity low, a recurrence with parents P1, P2, HIPPO-3, and
  CEREBELLUM-2): the cortisol channel is a cross-stratal skip - community-and-society
  (ordinal 14) down to macromolecular (ordinal 4) - with no modelled intervening
  hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal (HPA) axis mechanism. The relation carries skips:true
  honestly and classifies as a clean skip (skipping, with an empty skip-gap list). The
  minimum remedy is to model the intervening strata as a chain of adjacent-stratum
  steps, or a first-class "managed seam" construct. It is re-engaged from the Wave 2
  slice's cortisol channel, not re-invented.

Both findings stay open and honestly recorded. The safety property (no undefined
valence) and the reentrant conditioning loop were both carried by the enforced
invariants - the membership contract made the guarantee a one-line declaration, and
the closure hybrid carried the loop - so those are the program working as designed,
not findings. The deliverable is the finding: especially the affect and appraisal
gap, and the cross-stratal cortisol seam.
