CONNECTOME-AMYGDALA ARCHITECTURE  (v1)

The structure of the region: its packs, the strata they map to, the layer
assignments, the layer-zero substrate, the Lattice coordination, the interfaces (the
ports and the appraisal conduit), the cross-stratal cortisol skip, and where it reuses
PrologAI read-only. Author: the PrologAI Community.


PART ONE - THE STRATUM-PRIMARY DECOMPOSITION

The region is carved one pack per Causalontology stratum on the outside, and one
construct per module on the inside (the Wave 3 verdict). It occupies five strata plus a
layer-zero substrate of two packs:

  layer 0  neural_lattice          substrate (no stratum)               stigmergy plus await/notify
  layer 0  causal_grounding        substrate (no stratum)               the Causalontology 3.0.0 minting vocabulary
  layer 1  macromolecular_stratum  stratum macromolecular (ordinal 4)   the cortisol (glucocorticoid) receptor cascade
  layer 2  cellular_stratum        stratum cellular (ordinal 6)         the amygdala projection neurons
  layer 3  synaptic_stratum        stratum synaptic (ordinal 7)         the lateral-amygdala associative plasticity write
  layer 4  region_stratum          stratum region (ordinal 9)           appraise, condition, modulate; the committed appraisal; the loop
  layer 5  community_stratum       stratum community_and_society (14)    the social source of cortisol (pure, no tick)

Seven packs, six layers. Each stratum pack declares BOTH its pack layer (in pack.pl,
for the L4 layer rule) and its stratum label (stratum(Label), for the N6 binding), and
the stratum's ordinal is read authoritatively from the pack's own minted stratum record
in structure/. The region OCCUPIES the cellular stratum (ordinal 6), which the Wave 6
memory region deliberately left empty, as the cerebellum did before it - appraisal is
genuinely a cell-level story (the lateral-amygdala input neurons and the central-nucleus
output neurons). Five strata are occupied - ordinals 4, 6, 7, 9, 14 - and their layers
fall out order-preserving (1, 2, 3, 4, 5), which the binding checker confirms with zero
violations.

The two substrate packs carry no stratum. They are layer(0) and are unbound gaps to the
N6 binding, never violations: neural_lattice is the coordination substrate and
causal_grounding is the shared minting vocabulary. Every record-owning pack co-locates
its structure with its runtime (the verdict's stratum-primary stance); together the
packs mint 32 Causalontology 3.0.0 records across eight kinds (assertion,
causal_relation_object, conduit, continuant, occurrent, port, realizable, stratum),
materialised as one JavaScript Object Notation (JSON) file each under structure/.


PART TWO - THE LAYER AND STRATUM LADDER, AND THE CORTISOL SKIP

The ladder runs coarse-and-social at the top (high ordinal, high layer) down to
molecular at the bottom (low ordinal, low layer). The cortisol channel is a single long
DOWNWARD edge on the right, from the community stratum straight to the macromolecular
stratum, skipping every stratum between:

    layer 5   community_and_society (14)   community_stratum        o   social stressor
                                                                    :     (skip source)
    layer 4   region (9)                   region_stratum           :
                                                                    :   cortisol descends,
    layer 3   synaptic (7)                 synaptic_stratum         :   skipping the region
                                                                    :   (9), synaptic (7),
    layer 2   cellular (6)                 cellular_stratum         :   and cellular (6)
                                                                    :   strata between
    layer 1   macromolecular (4)           macromolecular_stratum   o   receptor activation
                                                                          (skip target)

    layer 0   neural_lattice               substrate (no stratum)   stigmergy plus await/notify
    layer 0   causal_grounding             substrate (no stratum)   the minting vocabulary

The skip's high endpoint is the social-stressor occurrent minted in community_stratum
(ordinal 14); its low endpoint is the receptor-activation occurrent minted in
macromolecular_stratum (ordinal 4). The channel itself is a causal relation object,
minted in the community pack, carrying skips:true. It descends from ordinal 14 to
ordinal 4, so it is a legal DOWNWARD cross-stratal edge (Part Seven explains why).


PART THREE - THE PACK GRAPH (STATIC IMPORTS, ALL DOWNWARD)

  region_stratum (4)
    |-- macromolecular_stratum (1)   the cortisol regime, threat gain, and reward suppression
    |-- cellular_stratum (2)         the sensory encoding and the bounded salience projection
    |-- synaptic_stratum (3)         the associative plasticity write for the conditioning step
    |-- causal_grounding (0)         minting the region's own eleven structure records
    |-- neural_lattice (0)           the reentrant conditioning loop's coordination
    |-- (external, read-only) PrologAI membership_contract, lattice, lists

  community_stratum (5)
    |-- macromolecular_stratum (1)   the skip's low endpoint (a downward reference, 14 to 4)
    |-- causal_grounding (0)         minting the community stratum's four records
    |-- (external, read-only) PrologAI causal_core, lists

  macromolecular_stratum (1) --> causal_grounding (0)
  cellular_stratum (2)       --> causal_grounding (0)
  synaptic_stratum (3)       --> causal_grounding (0)

Every intra-region import is to a strictly lower layer; there is no upward edge, so the
strict layer rule passes. Two facts guard the layering deliberately. First, the
region_stratum pack does NOT import community_stratum (layer 5, above it): the social
source of cortisol is upstream, and the region reads only the transduced regime and gain
down from the macromolecular cascade, so no upward edge is created. Second, the
community_stratum pack (layer 5) imports the macromolecular pack (layer 1) DOWNWARD for
the skip's low endpoint, and never imports the region - so it adds no upward edge either.


PART FOUR - THE LAYER-ZERO SUBSTRATE AND THE LATTICE COORDINATION

Both substrate packs are reused verbatim. neural_lattice is the slice's realisation of
the closure rule: stigmergy for state plus notification for reactivity. It opens a single
PrologAI Lattice nexus (a locus:// address), posts and awaits numbered PHASE CUES, and
records the glass-box hop trace. A cue is a numbered positional slot (phase_1, phase_2),
never the name of who picks it up; a waiter blocks on a private message queue with no
busy-poll until a Lattice write wakes it. neural_lattice imports only PrologAI's
library(lattice), so its layer(0) has no intra-region upward edge. causal_grounding
holds the fourteen shared minting helpers (cm_stratum, cm_occ, cm_cnt, cm_rlz, cm_port,
cm_conduit, cm_cro, cm_signed_assertion_over, and the rest), reused byte-identically from
the Wave 2 slice so a record minted here has the same content-addressed id as the same
record in the slice; it imports only PrologAI's causal_core, signing, and sha.

The reentrant conditioning loop's only shared state is the loop snapshot, carried on the
Lattice as a state dictionary. It rides two phases. Phase 1 is the TRIAL cue: the tick
awaits it, does one appraise-and-condition step, and re-posts the next phase-1 cue
(reentry). Phase 2 is the RESULT cue: when the association has settled or the trial
budget is spent, the tick posts the outcome to phase 2 instead of re-entering. State
lives in the environment, not in an actor's stack.


PART FIVE - THE RUNTIME (ONE ACTOR, REENTRANT THROUGH THE LATTICE)

The region has exactly one runtime actor: the conditioning tick,
region_stratum_condition_tick/1, driven by the runner as a PrologAI cyclic_actor (a
background thread). It awaits the phase-1 trial cue, appraises the cue at the current
association and commits it through the once-mode membership contract (so the
no-undefined-valence guarantee holds on EVERY trial), does one conditioning step, and
re-posts either the next phase-1 cue (reentry) or the phase-2 result. The learned
association changes monotonically under a steady schedule; the runner distinguishes three
outcomes - CONVERGED (the association settles within tolerance), BOUNDED-STOP (an
alternating partial-reinforcement schedule that reaches the trial bound without settling,
a legal "not fully conditioned"), and never HANGS (the driver's phase-2 await is bounded,
so a hang FAILS rather than blocks).

The macromolecular, cellular, synaptic, and community packs are PURE: they carry no tick
and never coordinate on the Lattice. The region invokes them DOWNWARD as sub-modules
during a step (the cortisol read, the sensory encoding, the synaptic write, the skip's
low endpoint), which is permitted structural reuse, not runtime coupling. The closure
checker proves this non-vacuously: it confirms zero actor-to-actor references, that the
tick reenters by re-posting a Lattice cue and never calls itself in-process, and that it
awaits on the Lattice rather than busy-polling; injecting a self-recursive tick makes the
checker exit 1.


PART SIX - THE INTERFACES BETWEEN PACKS

There are three interface surfaces: the downward runtime calls, the minted ports and
appraisal conduit, and the membership-contract seam.

The downward runtime calls. To appraise, the region reads the stimulus's innate
threat/reward feature from cellular_stratum_encode/2 and clamps the resulting drive to a
bounded salience with cellular_stratum_project/2; it reads the cortisol regime, the
threat gain, and the reward suppression from macromolecular_stratum_cortisol_regime/2,
macromolecular_stratum_threat_gain/2, and macromolecular_stratum_reward_suppression/2; an
UNKNOWN stimulus (not encoded by the cellular neurons) makes the appraisal fail so the
region abstains with the explicit no_appraisal. To condition, region_stratum_condition/3
delegates to synaptic_stratum_potentiate/3 (a paired trial strengthens the association
toward 1.0 by a half-gap Rescorla-Wagner step; an unpaired trial extinguishes it toward
0.0).

The ports and the appraisal conduit. The region mints a stimulus_input port that accepts
two references - the cellular sensory-encoding occurrent and the synaptic plasticity
occurrent - and an appraisal_output port that emits the affective-appraisal occurrent.
Between them it mints the appraisal conduit: a COMPUTATIONAL conduit (it carries a
transform, asserting it computes an appraisal rather than merely transmitting), whose
transform is the region-internal appraisal causal relation object (the fear-conditioning
occurrent causes the affective-appraisal occurrent, both at the region stratum, so this
relation spans no strata). The macromolecular pack mints its own internal modulation
causal relation object (receptor activation causes appraisal modulation, both at ordinal
4). The only cross-stratal relation is the cortisol skip, owned by the community pack.

The membership-contract seam. The committed appraisal is guarded by PrologAI's
membership_contract construct, DECLARED not hand-rolled. region_stratum_commit_appraisal/2
is a deliberately thin identity that returns the proposed appraisal; the guarantee comes
from the declaration

  :- membership_contract_enforce_goal(region_stratum_commit_appraisal/2, 2,
          region_stratum_legal_appraisal, no_appraisal, once).

which names the legal set by a membership-TEST goal (the accessor form, N11) rather than a
list, and checks the committed single answer deterministically (once mode, N14). The
accessor region_stratum_legal_appraisal/1 accepts appraisal(Valence, Salience, Regime)
when the salience is a number in the unit interval and the valence is legal in its regime
(region_stratum_valence_in_regime/2: all four valences at baseline; appetitive suppressed
under stress). Because the legal set is context-dependent on the cortisol regime and
carries a continuous salience, it is neither a fixed list nor countable - which is exactly
why the never-materialised accessor form is required.


PART SEVEN - THE CORTISOL CROSS-STRATAL SKIP, A LEGAL DOWNWARD EDGE

The cortisol channel is re-engaged, not re-invented, from the Wave 2 slice: the same
shape, minted fresh here. It is a causal relation object owned by community_stratum
(community_stratum_cortisol_skip_cro/1): the social-stressor occurrent at the
community-and-society stratum (ordinal 14) causes the receptor-activation occurrent at the
macromolecular stratum (ordinal 4), carrying skips:true. The relation does not model the
intervening endocrine pathway (the hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal axis), so it declares
the skip honestly rather than pretending to a mechanism it does not have.

Two independent checks accept it. The N6 binding checker passes it because it is a
DOWNWARD edge - it runs from a high ordinal (14) to a low ordinal (4); only an upward
cross-stratal edge, which would let a fine stratum silently drive a coarse one, would
fail. The structure validator's skip classifier (causal_core_classify plus
causal_core_skip_gaps, exercised by community_stratum_skip_check/2) classifies it as
skipping with an empty skip-gaps list - because skips:true is set, the absent
intervening mechanism is a declared, positive finding, not an unexplained gap. A
provenance assertion over the skip is minted and Ed25519-signed, and the validator
verifies that signature. The unmodelled intervening mechanism is recorded honestly as the
LEDGER finding AMYGDALA-2, a recurrence of the same unmanaged cross-stratal seam the
slice, the memory region, and the cerebellum each recorded; it is re-engaged deliberately,
not closed.


PART EIGHT - READ-ONLY REUSE OF PROLOGAI

PrologAI is reused at a pinned commit through the PROLOGAI_HOME environment variable
(default /home/ccaitwo/PrologAI), exactly as the arbiter, the memory region, and the
cerebellum did - by checkout, no vendoring and no fork. The region reuses, read-only,
PrologAI's lattice (the stigmergy plus await/notify substrate), actors (the cyclic_actor
thread that drives the tick), membership_contract (the N8 contract, its N11 accessor form,
and the Wave 8 N14 once mode that the committed appraisal declares), causal_core and the
signing harness (the Causalontology 3.0.0 engine and Ed25519 signing the stratum packs
load through causal_grounding), and layer (the N6 binding the gate script runs). Each gate
resolves PROLOGAI_HOME, guards that PrologAI - and specifically the construct it needs, the
membership_contract module or the layer pack - is reachable, and stops with a clear message
otherwise. The region minted, validated, and gated against PrologAI main at commit
a1b23432c6a357b8768422daa41a3abcbcf52de2 (Wave 10 Stage 1, the Causalontology 3.0.0 adoption). PrologAI is
never modified.
