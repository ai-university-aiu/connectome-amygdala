CONNECTOME-AMYGDALA REFINEMENT  (v1)

How the design was verified and improved: the checkers, the in-pack tests, the
adversarial no-undefined-valence battery, the gates, and the one bug found and
fixed during the build.
Author: the PrologAI Community.


PART ONE - THE GATES AS ADVERSARIAL CHECKS

The flagship gate is NO UNDEFINED VALENCE (bin/check_membership.sh, over
bin/check_membership.pl): an adversarial battery that TRIES to make the region
commit an appraisal whose valence lies outside the legal set, and confirms it
cannot. It runs in two arms. The DIRECT arm feeds a fixed list of candidate
appraisals straight at the guarded commit: seven legal appraisals across both
regimes, the explicit no_appraisal abstention, four invented affective tags
(euphoria, terror_supreme, xyzzy, rage), two out-of-range saliences (5.0 and
-0.1), two contextually-illegal appraisals (appetitive under stress, at two
saliences), and one malformed regime (euphoric_regime). The PIPELINE arm runs
the real appraise-then-commit path over a wide sweep - every stimulus crossed
with five cortisol levels and five learned associations - and confirms every
appraisal the region would actually produce is legal. Each commit is classified
as member (a legal appraisal), no_appraisal (the safe abstention), refused (the
once-mode membership contract threw), or ESCAPED (an out-of-set appraisal
committed WITHOUT a throw - the one outcome the guarantee forbids). The battery
inspects 192 attempts, records 9 out-of-set refusals, and finds 0 escapes. The
9 refusals partition exactly as the direct arm intends: 4 invented tags, 2
out-of-range saliences, 2 appetitive-under-stress appraisals, and 1 malformed
regime. Because region_stratum_commit_appraisal/2 DECLARES PrologAI's membership
contract in the accessor form (a context-dependent, uncountable legal-appraisal
test goal, never materialised) and in once mode, every refusal is the contract's,
not a hand-rolled guard's. This is what makes the gate meaningful: the refusal of
an invented tag, an out-of-range salience, and a contextually-illegal appraisal
all flow from the same one-line declaration.

The structure validator (bin/validate_structure.sh, over
bin/validate_structure.pl) assembles the region's 32 newly-minted records from the
five stratum packs and validates every one against PrologAI's unmodified
Causalontology 3.0.0 conformance engine - the JavaScript Object Notation (JSON)
schema check (co_validate_schema/4) and the local semantic rules
(causal_core_validate_semantics/3) - across 8 record kinds (assertion,
causal_relation_object, conduit, continuant, occurrent, port, realizable, and
stratum). It then confirms the cross-stratal cortisol relation classifies as
SKIPPING with an empty skip-gap list (community-and-society 14 down to
macromolecular 4, carrying skips:true honestly about the un-modelled
hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal pathway), and that the Ed25519 signed provenance
assertion over that relation VERIFIES. Each record is also written to
structure/<name>.json as an artifact.

The layer rule L4 (bin/check_layers.sh) loads PrologAI's unmodified library(layer)
and confirms zero upward edges among the 7 declared packs (layers 0, 0, 1, 2, 3,
4, 5). The N6 binding checker (bin/check_layer_binding.sh) confirms the 5 bound
stratum packs are order-preserving against their stratum ordinals (4, 6, 7, 9,
and 14 map to layers 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5) with 0 binding violations; the two
substrate packs (neural_lattice and causal_grounding) declare no stratum and are
unbound gaps, never violations. Critically, the DOWNWARD cortisol skip passes the
binding check: only an upward edge would fail, and a legal downward skip is not
one.

The closure checker (bin/check_no_coupling.sh) confirms the region's one runtime
actor - the conditioning tick, region_stratum_condition_tick - reenters through
the Lattice and never through the call stack. It strips comments from every
stratum pack and checks three things: that no stratum pack names another
stratum's runtime tick (0 actor-to-actor references, the four pure packs being
downward sub-modules, not coupled actors); that the tick re-posts a Lattice cue
(neural_lattice_post_cue) and calls itself at most once, at its own head (no
in-process recursion); and that the tick awaits on the Lattice
(neural_lattice_await_cue) rather than busy-polling. The check is proven
NON-VACUOUS: injecting a self-recursive tick - a second in-process call site -
makes the checker exit 1, so a passing run is a real result, not a checker that
can never fail.


PART TWO - THE IN-PACK TEST SUITES

Each pack ships a Prolog Unit (PLUnit) suite, run by bin/run_tests.sh, each in its
own SWI-Prolog process. The seven suites all pass: causal_grounding 2,
neural_lattice 2, cellular_stratum 5, synaptic_stratum 5, community_stratum 5,
macromolecular_stratum 6, and region_stratum 14. The region suite is the deepest,
and proves the region's promises in isolation: that an aversive stimulus is
appraised threat with high salience; that an appetitive stimulus is appraised
appetitive at baseline but NOT under stress (cortisol suppresses the reward
appraisal); that cortisol amplifies threat (a part-conditioned cue reads safe at
rest but threat under stress); that an unknown stimulus yields the explicit
no_appraisal; that the legal valence set genuinely narrows under stress
(appetitive is legal at baseline and illegal under stress, while threat is legal
in both); that a legal appraisal and the abstention both commit, while an invented
valence, an out-of-range salience, and appetitive-under-stress are each REFUSED by
the once-mode accessor contract with a membership_contract_goal_violation; that
the committed appraisal is deterministic (exactly one solution, the once mode);
that a paired conditioning step strengthens the association monotonically and a
repeatedly paired neutral cue eventually reads threat; and that all 11 of the
region's structure records are schema-valid, including that the computational
appraisal conduit carries a transform. The macromolecular, cellular, synaptic, and
community suites each prove their native dynamics (the regime classifier, the
threat gain and reward suppression, the sensory encoding and bounded projection,
the associative potentiation and extinction, the cortisol skip classification and
signature) and their structure records in the same isolated style.


PART THREE - THE BUG FOUND AND FIXED - THE SYNAPTIC POTENTIATION CHOICEPOINT

During the build the associative write left a spurious choicepoint.
synaptic_stratum_potentiate/3 has two clauses - one for a paired trial (the
second argument true) and one for an unpaired trial (false) - and SWI-Prolog's
first-argument indexing keys on the FIRST argument, the association (a number),
not on the pairing flag that actually distinguishes the clauses. So after the
correct clause produced its answer, a choicepoint remained, and PLUnit reported
the region and synaptic tests "succeeded with choicepoint". This is a
determinism defect, not a wrong answer: the write was correct, but not clean. The
fix is a green cut - number(Assoc0), ! at the head of each clause - which
confirms the current strength is a number and then commits to the clause the
pairing selects, making the write deterministic. Because the two clauses were
already mutually exclusive on their second argument, the cut removes only the dead
alternative and changes no result: the acquisition and extinction sequences, and
every downstream appraisal, are byte-identical before and after. No behaviour
change.


PART FOUR - THE MONOTONICITY AND CONVERGENCE STORY

Fear conditioning is a bounded associative rule: a paired trial closes half the
remaining gap to the ceiling (a Rescorla-Wagner half-gap step at learning rate
0.5, association toward 1.0), and an unpaired trial decays the association halfway
toward the floor (toward 0.0). Under a STEADY schedule the association therefore
changes MONOTONICALLY, and the reentrant loop distinguishes three outcomes, which
the runner (bin/run_amygdala.sh) tells apart. ACQUISITION (a neutral tone always
paired) converges to threat after 5 trials: the association climbs 0.0, 0.5, 0.75,
0.875, 0.9375, 0.969, the per-trial change falls below the settle tolerance of
0.05, and the cue's committed appraisal flips from neutral to threat. EXTINCTION
(a conditioned tone always unpaired) converges to neutral after 5 trials, the
association decaying from 0.9 to 0.028. PARTIAL reinforcement (an alternating
schedule that pairs on even trials and omits on odd ones) never settles, so it
BOUNDED-STOPS at the trial bound of 8 with the association oscillating near 0.33 -
a legal "not fully conditioned", distinguished from a hang by a bounded await
(30 seconds) that FAILS rather than blocks. Across the three episodes the loop
records 21 conditioning hops, every one arriving through the Lattice (the tick
re-posts its own cue), with 0 actor-to-actor references and no busy-poll. The
runner also re-checks the safety property live: it appraises a spread of stimuli
(showing all four valences, the abstention, and the cortisol modulation) and then
adversarially forces an invented valence (euphoria) and a contextually-illegal one
(appetitive under stress) past the commit, both of which the contract REFUSES. Its
verdict is pass.


PART FIVE - THE MINI REGRESSION, IN HONEST FORM

The build is gated additionally by PrologAI's own mini regression
($PROLOGAI_HOME/bin/run_mini_regression.sh), a ten-percent spot-check of the
Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus for Artificial General Intelligence (ARC-AGI)
benchmarks. It reports ARC-AGI-1 40/40 and ARC-AGI-2 12/12. These are the honest
figures: forty of four hundred and twelve of one hundred twenty, a spot-check, not
the full run. The full regression stays DEFERRED, and this result is NEVER rounded
up to 400/400 or 120/120. The region reuses PrologAI read-only at the pinned
commit a1b23432c6a357b8768422daa41a3abcbcf52de2 (main, Wave 10 Stage 1, the
Causalontology 3.0.0 adoption); PrologAI was not modified, so the mini regression is a guard that
the region's checkout did not disturb the substrate it rests on, not a claim about
the region's own reasoning.
