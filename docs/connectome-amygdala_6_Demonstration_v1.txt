CONNECTOME-AMYGDALA DEMONSTRATION  (v1)

A plan of intent for demonstrating the region: what to run, what it shows, and what a
viewer should conclude. This is a plan, not a finished demonstration, and it changes no
code. Author: the PrologAI Community.


PART ONE - WHAT IS BEING DEMONSTRATED

connectome-amygdala is a valence and salience region: its job is to APPRAISE - to assign
a stimulus a valence (its affective tag) and a salience (how much it matters), to learn
threat by association, and to modulate that appraisal by cortisol. It is distinct from
the earlier regions, which selected (arbiter), stored (hippocampus), and corrected
(cerebellum); this is the first region that has to hold an affective judgement. The
demonstration exists to make three claims visible and checkable by a viewer who does not
take them on trust: that the region APPRAISES with genuine affect (all four valences, the
explicit abstention, and cortisol modulation); that it LEARNS threat by association (a
neutral cue acquires a threat appraisal over trials, and later extinguishes); and that it
can NEVER emit an undefined valence (the once-mode accessor contract refuses an invented
tag, an out-of-range salience, and a contextually-illegal appraisal). A fourth thing is
demonstrated by not being hidden: the region's defining function, appraisal, presses on a
real limit of the platform - PrologAI has no first-class construct for a persisted,
modulatory affective state - which the program records in its Ledger and in the shape of
its own data rather than papering over.


PART TWO - WHAT TO RUN

TRACK ONE - THE APPRAISAL DEMO. Run bin/run_amygdala.sh with PROLOGAI_HOME pointing at a
PrologAI checkout at or after the once-mode commit
(a1b23432c6a357b8768422daa41a3abcbcf52de2). Its first section appraises a spread of seven
probe stimuli and commits each through the contract.
  What it shows: every one of the four valences appears - pain reads threat, food at rest
  reads appetitive, an untrained tone reads neutral, and a part-conditioned tone at rest
  reads safe (wary) - together with the explicit no-appraisal on an unknown stimulus, so
  the region abstains rather than inventing a tag. Two probes make the cortisol modulation
  visible: food under stress reads neutral, not appetitive, because reward is suppressed
  while cortisol is high; and the same part-conditioned tone, appraised under stress,
  reads threat, because cortisol amplifies the threat drive. Each appraisal is narrated
  glass-box: the stimulus, the cortisol level, the learned association, and the committed
  appraisal.

TRACK TWO - THE CONDITIONING EPISODES. The same runner then spawns the conditioning actor
and runs three fixed episodes through the reentrant loop (appraise, condition,
re-appraise).
  What it shows: acquisition pairs a neutral tone with an aversive outcome and CONVERGES
  to threat at five trials, the learned association climbing monotonically from 0.0 to
  0.969 as the cue's appraisal flips from neutral to threat; extinction presents a
  conditioned tone unpaired and CONVERGES back to neutral at five trials, the association
  falling from 0.9 to 0.028; and a partial-reinforcement episode, whose schedule
  alternates paired and unpaired trials, never settles and BOUNDED-STOPS at eight trials
  with the association near 0.33 - a legal "not fully conditioned", not a hang. Twenty-one
  conditioning hops occur, every one arriving through the Lattice: the tick awaits its
  numbered cue, does one step, and re-posts its own cue, so the loop reenters by
  notification and never by in-process recursion. The runner tells the three outcomes
  apart - converged, bounded-stopped, and (never seen) hung, which a bounded await would
  report as a failure rather than block on forever.

TRACK THREE - NO UNDEFINED VALENCE. Run bin/check_membership.sh (the flagship adversarial
battery) and read the adversarial line in the runner.
  What it shows: across 192 committed appraisals - a direct sweep of hand-built
  candidates plus the real appraise-then-commit pipeline over many stimuli, cortisol
  levels, and learned associations - not one escapes the legal valence set. Nine
  out-of-set attempts are refused by the once-mode membership contract: invented affective
  tags (euphoria, rage, xyzzy, terror_supreme), an out-of-range salience (5.0 and -0.1),
  a contextually-illegal appraisal (appetitive under stress), and a malformed regime. The
  guarantee is obtained by a single declaration - the contract in its accessor form (a
  membership-test goal, so the context-dependent, uncountable legal set is never
  materialised) and in once mode (one appraisal per stimulus, checked deterministically) -
  not by a hand-rolled guard.

TRACK FOUR - THE GATES. Run each bin/*.sh checker and PrologAI's mini regression.
  What it shows: the structure validator (bin/validate_structure.sh) confirms 32
  newly-minted records valid against Causalontology 3.0.0 across eight kinds (assertion,
  causal_relation_object, conduit, continuant, occurrent, port, realizable, and stratum),
  that the cortisol skip classifies as skipping with an empty skip-gap list, and that the
  Ed25519 signed assertion over that skip verifies. The layer rule (bin/check_layers.sh)
  passes seven declared packs with zero upward edges; the binding checker
  (bin/check_layer_binding.sh) passes five bound packs whose stratum ordinals (4, 6, 7, 9,
  14) map order-preserving to layers (1, 2, 3, 4, 5), two unbound substrate packs, and
  zero binding violations, with the downward cortisol skip explicitly allowed. The closure
  checker (bin/check_no_coupling.sh) passes and is proven non-vacuous - injecting a
  self-recursive tick makes it exit 1 - with exactly one runtime actor
  (region_stratum_condition_tick), zero actor-to-actor references, no in-process
  recursion, and no busy-poll. Every pack's in-pack PLUnit suite passes. PrologAI's
  benchmark spot-check is unmoved at forty of forty and twelve of twelve.

TRACK FIVE - THE FINDING. Read LEDGER.md, entries AMYGDALA-1 and AMYGDALA-2, and inspect
the Regime field of structure/cro_appraisal.json and the skips flag of
structure/cro_cortisol_skip.json (these are JavaScript Object Notation, or JSON, records
written by the validator).
  What it shows: AMYGDALA-1, the headline, is visible in the data itself. The committed
  appraisal is the triple appraisal(Valence, Salience, Regime), not the pair a valence and
  a salience would suggest, because the membership contract's accessor is called
  once(call(TestGoal, Out)) and receives only the committed value, never a context - so to
  make the legal valence set depend on the cortisol regime the region must smuggle that
  regime into the committed term. The learned association and the cortisol tone, likewise,
  are carried as ordinary fields on the Lattice conditioning-loop state, not as a
  first-class held affect. AMYGDALA-2 is visible in the skip record: the cortisol channel
  jumps the community-and-society stratum (ordinal 14) to the macromolecular stratum
  (ordinal 4) carrying skips:true, with no modelled hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal (HPA)
  axis between - an honest cross-stratal skip, re-engaged from the Wave 2 slice's channel,
  not re-invented.


PART THREE - WHAT A VIEWER SHOULD CONCLUDE

A viewer should conclude that an appraising region can be built glass-box on the enforced
invariants; that its safety property (no undefined valence) is a language guarantee rather
than hand-rolled code, even though the legal set is context-dependent on cortisol and
uncountable in its continuous salience; that the region genuinely learns threat by
association and genuinely unlearns it, monotonically and through the Lattice; and that
where the platform still lacks a construct - a first-class, persisted, modulatory
affective state - the region says so, in the Ledger and in the smuggled Regime field of
its own committed appraisals, rather than pretending it is there. The point is not only
that the region appraises; it is that the affect it computes can, for now, only be
DESCRIBED as data, and the build records exactly that.


PART FOUR - WHAT IT MUST NOT CLAIM

It must not claim the mini regression as a full benchmark score; the honest figures are
forty of forty and twelve of twelve, never rounded to 400/400 or 120/120. It must not
claim that affect is modelled as a first-class held state - it is folded into the
committed term and carried as ordinary Lattice data, and the finding AMYGDALA-1 is open.
It must not claim the cortisol channel models the endocrine pathway - it is an honest
downward cross-stratal skip flagged skips:true with no modelled HPA axis, and the finding
AMYGDALA-2 stays open. It demonstrates one region, not the full connectome, and it
modifies no code - not PrologAI, not the earlier regions. Where the region cannot be
faithful, it says so, in the Ledger and in the data.
