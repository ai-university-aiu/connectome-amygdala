CONNECTOME-AMYGDALA SPECIFICATION  (v1)

This is the first of six documents describing the connectome-amygdala repository.
The five Specification, Pseudocode, Architecture, Refinement, and Completion files
follow the SPARC methodology; a sixth Demonstration file is a plan of intent.
Author: the PrologAI Community.

Acronyms are spelled out in full on first use, with the acronym in parentheses, and
the bare acronym thereafter. External proper names are kept verbatim (SWI-Prolog,
Ed25519, JavaScript Object Notation, Git).


PART ONE - PURPOSE

connectome-amygdala is a valence and salience region: the first Connectome region
whose job is to APPRAISE. Where the earlier regions selected (connectome-arbiter),
stored (connectome-hippocampus), and corrected against a taught error
(connectome-cerebellum), this region assigns a stimulus an affective meaning. It gives
the stimulus a VALENCE (an affective tag) and a SALIENCE (how much it matters), it
learns threat by association (fear conditioning), and it modulates the appraisal by
cortisol - and it makes the appraisal it commits provably safe, so the region can never
emit a valence outside the defined set.

It is Wave 8, Part Three of the Connectome program. It is independent of Part Two (the
cerebellum) - neither blocks the other - but both rest on Part One, PrologAI's Wave 8
once-deterministic membership mode. Its dynamics (affective appraisal and associative
fear learning) are genuinely distinct from selection, storage, and supervised
correction, and that distinctness is the point: an appraising region is the one that
forces contact with a construct the earlier regions never needed - a held, modulatory
affective state.


PART TWO - FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

FR-1 APPRAISE. Given a stimulus, a cortisol level, and the current learned association,
the region produces a valence (its affective tag: threat, safe, appetitive, or neutral)
and a salience (a bounded rate in the range 0.0 to 1.0). The appraisal combines the
stimulus's innate threat and reward feature (read down from the cellular projection
neurons) with the learned association, modulated by cortisol.

FR-2 ABSTAIN ON THE UNKNOWN. A stimulus the cellular neurons do not encode yields the
explicit no-appraisal. The region abstains; it never invents an affective tag for a
stimulus it cannot read.

FR-3 CONDITION AND EXTINGUISH. The region learns threat by association: a previously
neutral cue paired with an aversive outcome acquires a threat association (lateral
amygdala long-term potentiation, or LTP), so it later evokes threat; an unpaired cue
extinguishes the association back toward zero. This is dynamics, kept native (an
associative rate law), never forced into a causal relation object (CRO).

FR-4 MODULATE BY CORTISOL. The region modulates the appraisal by a circulating cortisol
level: a cortisol level classifies into a baseline or a stress regime (threshold 0.5),
a threat gain rises with cortisol (gain equals 1.0 plus the cortisol level), and reward
appraisal is suppressed under stress (a reward factor of 1.0 at baseline, 0.0 under
stress).

FR-5 NO UNDEFINED VALENCE. The committed appraisal must name a valence in the legal set,
or be the explicit no-appraisal; it may never carry a valence outside the defined set (an
invented affective tag) or a malformed salience. This is the safety property.

FR-6 CONTEXT-DEPENDENT LEGALITY. The legal valence set depends on the cortisol regime.
At baseline all four valences are legal; under stress the appetitive (reward and approach)
appraisal is suppressed and is not a legal tag. Cortisol narrows the set. Because the set
is context-dependent AND the salience is a continuous real, the legal set is neither a
fixed list nor even countable.

FR-7 COMMIT ONE APPRAISAL. The region commits exactly one appraisal per stimulus, guarded
so the committed appraisal is a member of the legal set (in its regime) or the explicit
no-appraisal, checked deterministically.

FR-8 THE CONDITIONING LOOP. The region runs a reentrant conditioning loop - appraise,
condition, re-appraise - coordinated through the shared Lattice memory. Under a steady
schedule the learned association changes monotonically. The loop converges (the
association settles), bounded-stops (an alternating partial-reinforcement schedule that
never settles, a legal "not fully conditioned"), or - never - hangs.


PART THREE - NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

NFR-1 GLASS-BOX NARRATION. Every appraisal, every conditioning trial, and every refusal
is narrated in plain language as it happens - the valence assigned, the association before
and after, the cortisol regime, the bounded-stop or convergence verdict - so a reader
watches the region reason rather than trusting a final answer.

NFR-2 STRATUM-PRIMARY DECOMPOSITION. Under the Wave 3 verdict the region is one pack per
Causalontology stratum on the outside, and one construct per module on the inside. The
cross-pack edges are the stratum edges; the internal sub-modules (appraise, condition,
modulate) are independently testable.

NFR-3 GROUNDING. Every structure record is grounded in Causalontology 3.0.0 and validates
against the standard's conformance engine; the appraisal and learning dynamics are kept
native (threshold and rate laws), never forced into a causal relation object.

NFR-4 THE STRICT LAYER RULE. Every pack declares its layer; no lower-layer pack depends on
a higher-layer one; the layer checker reports zero upward edges.

NFR-5 THE LAYER-TO-STRATUM BINDING. Every stratum pack declares the Causalontology stratum
it represents; the binding checker confirms each pack's layer is order-preserving with its
stratum's ordinal.

NFR-6 THE CLOSURE DISCIPLINE. State lives on the Lattice (stigmergy); reactivity is a
blocking, bounded await woken by a write (no busy-poll); there are zero actor-to-actor
references and no in-process recursion.

NFR-7 THE SAFETY INVARIANT - DECLARED, NOT HAND-ROLLED. No undefined valence is enforced by
DECLARING PrologAI's membership contract in its accessor form (a membership-test goal, so
the uncountable legal set is never materialised) and in once mode (the single committed
appraisal, checked deterministically). Nothing is hand-rolled.

NFR-8 READ-ONLY REUSE. PrologAI is reused at a pinned commit through the PROLOGAI_HOME
environment variable; PrologAI is never modified. Each gate stops with a clear message if
PrologAI - or specifically the membership_contract construct - is not reachable.

NFR-9 ENGLISH-READABLE CODE. Every line of code in every Prolog file carries one plain
English comment immediately above it, per the English-Readable-Code (ERC) rule, including
the pack manifests and the test suites.


PART FOUR - OBJECTIVES AND CONSTRAINTS

The region's function is appraisal - assigning affective meaning and learning threat by
association - which is genuinely distinct from the dopamine reward-prediction-error of the
vertical slice, from the memory region's store-and-recall, and from the cerebellum's
supervised correction. That distinctness is the objective: an appraisal is not a one-shot
output but an affective STATE (a valence and a salience) that persists and colours later
processing, and whose very legality depends on a persisted context (the cortisol regime).
Building the region is the way to find out whether PrologAI can express such a state. The
headline finding (AMYGDALA-1) is that it cannot: there is no first-class construct for a
held, modulatory affective state.

Constraints: touch only the strata the region's constructs occupy; do not modify PrologAI,
Mentova, the frozen prologai-loops spike, connectome-proto-agi, the three frozen Wave 3
arms, connectome-arbiter, connectome-hippocampus, or connectome-cerebellum; do not modify
the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus for Artificial General Intelligence (ARC-AGI) solving
core in PrologAI; do not hand-roll the membership guarantee; do not weaken a gate; do not
scale to the full connectome or become a general framework. A gap the region hits is a
Ledger entry, not a change to PrologAI.


PART FIVE - ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA (THE GATES)

AC-1 NO UNDEFINED VALENCE. Every committed appraisal is a member of the legal valence set
(in its cortisol regime) or the explicit no-appraisal; an invented tag, an out-of-range
salience, and a contextually-illegal appraisal (appetitive under stress) are each refused;
the once-mode contract holds on every commit. The adversarial battery inspects 192 attempts
with 9 out-of-set refusals and 0 escapes. This is the first, non-negotiable gate.

AC-2 PACK TESTS. Every pack's in-pack PLUnit suite passes: the shared minting substrate 2,
the neural_lattice 2, the cellular_stratum 5, the synaptic_stratum 5, the
macromolecular_stratum 6, the community_stratum 5, and the region_stratum 14
(thirty-nine test blocks in total).

AC-3 STRUCTURE. The region's 32 newly-minted Causalontology 3.0.0 records validate across
eight kinds (assertion, causal relation object, conduit, continuant, occurrent, port,
realizable, stratum); the cross-stratal cortisol relation classifies as a clean skip
(skipping, with an empty skip-gaps list); and the Ed25519-signed provenance over the
cortisol skip verifies.

AC-4 LAYER AND BINDING. The layer checker reports zero upward edges among the 7 declared
packs (layers 0, 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5). The binding checker confirms the 5 bound packs are
order-preserving with their stratum ordinals (4, 6, 7, 9, 14 map to layers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5),
with the 2 substrate packs left honestly unbound and 0 binding violations; the downward
cortisol skip passes (only an upward edge would fail).

AC-5 CLOSURE. Coordination is through the Lattice, with zero actor-to-actor references and
no busy-poll; the one runtime actor is the conditioning tick; the check is proven
non-vacuous (injecting a self-recursive tick makes it fail).

AC-6 THE CONDITIONING LOOP. The loop converges or bounded-stops, never hangs, and the
association changes monotonically: acquisition (a paired tone) converges to threat at 5
trials as the association climbs from 0.0 to 0.969 and the cue flips from neutral to threat;
extinction (a conditioned tone unpaired) converges to neutral at 5 trials as the association
decays from 0.9 to 0.028; a partial (alternating) schedule bounded-stops at 8 trials with the
association near 0.33; the adversarial invented and contextually-illegal valences are refused.
All 21 conditioning hops arrive through the Lattice.

AC-7 MINI REGRESSION. Via PrologAI's own harness, the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus for
Artificial General Intelligence, generation one (ARC-AGI-1) forty of forty and generation two
(ARC-AGI-2) twelve of twelve, in the honest ten-percent form. The full regression stays
deferred; this is never rounded up to four hundred of four hundred or one hundred twenty of
one hundred twenty.


PART SIX - REFLECTION

The chief design choice is to let the enforced invariants carry the safety property. The
committed appraisal is one answer against a legal set that is both context-dependent (the
cortisol regime) and uncountable (the continuous salience), so declaring the membership
contract in its accessor form (a membership-test goal, never materialised) and in once mode
(the single committed answer, checked deterministically) is exactly the right fit - no
hand-rolled guard.

The chief predicted risk was affect itself, and the region pressed on it as expected.
PrologAI has no first-class construct for a persisted, modulatory affective state, so the
region has to work around the absence on two fronts. First, the membership contract - even
its accessor form - judges the OUTPUT value alone; its test goal is called with only the
committed value and cannot receive context, so to make the legal valence set depend on the
cortisol regime the region must SMUGGLE the regime into the committed value (the appraisal is
the triple of valence, salience, and regime rather than valence and salience). Second, the
persisted affect (the learned association, the cortisol tone) is carried as ordinary Lattice
and module data, plain fields on the conditioning-loop state, not as a first-class held
affect. Both are honest workarounds and both are recorded as workarounds (AMYGDALA-1). A
second, familiar wall recurs: the cortisol channel is a cross-stratal skip from the
community-and-society stratum (ordinal 14) to the macromolecular stratum (ordinal 4) with no
modelled hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal (HPA) axis mechanism between; it carries a skips-true
flag honestly and classifies clean (AMYGDALA-2, a recurrence). The region runs and its
no-undefined-valence guarantee holds, but the deliverable is the finding: the region records
the wall rather than papering over it.
